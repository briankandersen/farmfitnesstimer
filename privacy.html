Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Farm Fitness Timer is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, harvest, transmit, or share any personal data, usage analytics, or tracking information.

2. Local Storage

All your workout presets, custom timers, and app settings are saved completely locally on your own device using local browser storage (localStorage). This data never leaves your device and is never sent to any external servers. If you clear your browser data or uninstall the app, your saved workouts will be deleted.

3. Third-Party Services

The app includes a voluntary donation button linked to PayPal. If you choose to support the development and make a donation, PayPal will process your transaction and may collect information according to their own Privacy Policy. Farm Fitness Timer does not have access to, nor do we store, any of your financial or payment details.

4. App Permissions

The app utilizes the "Wake Lock" API (Screen functionality) to prevent your device's screen from turning off or going to sleep during an active workout. This permission is strictly used to keep the timer visible and does not access any personal information, camera, or microphone data.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We advise you to review this page periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at: brian@bodyhit.dk
